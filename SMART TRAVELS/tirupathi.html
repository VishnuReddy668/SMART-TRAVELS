About Tirupati

Tirupati, nestled in the foothills of the Tirumala Hills, is a renowned pilgrimage destination in India. It is home to the Sri Venkateswara Temple, one of the most sacred Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu.

Temples

  • Sri Venkateswara Temple
  • Sri Padmavati Ammavari Temple
  • Sri Kapileswara Temple
  • Sri Govindaraja Temple

Attractions

  • Tirumala Hills
  • Alipiri Hills
  • Srivari Museum
  • Thimmamma Marri Mandir

How to Reach

Tirupati is well-connected by road, rail, and air. Renigunta Railway Station and Tirupati Airport serve the city.
