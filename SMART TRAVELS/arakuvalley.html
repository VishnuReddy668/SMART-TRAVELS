About Araku Valley

Araku Valley, nestled in the Eastern Ghats of Andhra Pradesh, is a picturesque hill station known for its lush greenery, cascading waterfalls, and tribal culture. The valley offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Attractions

  • Borra Caves
  • Katiki Waterfalls
  • Anantagiri Hills
  • Tyda Park
  • Tribal Museum
  • Coffee Plantations

How to Reach

Araku Valley is well-connected by road and rail. The nearest airport is Visakhapatnam.
