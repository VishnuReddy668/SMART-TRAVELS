About Amaravathi

Amaravathi is the planned capital city of the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. Located on the banks of the Krishna River, it is being developed as a sustainable and smart city. Amaravathi aims to be a global hub for business, technology, and culture.

Attractions

  • Krishna River
  • Buddha Vanam Park
  • Amaravathi Buddhist Site
  • Amaravathi Botanical Garden
  • Amaravathi Secretariat Building

How to Reach

Amaravathi is well-connected by road. The nearest airport is Vijayawada, and the nearest railway station is Guntur.
