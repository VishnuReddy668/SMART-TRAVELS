About Puttaparthi

Puttaparthi, a small village in the Anantapur district of Andhra Pradesh, is a renowned spiritual center. It is the birthplace and home of Sathya Sai Baba, a revered spiritual leader and philanthropist.

Sai Baba

Sathya Sai Baba, a spiritual leader and humanitarian, gained worldwide recognition for his teachings and philanthropic activities. His ashram in Puttaparthi, Prasanthi Nilayam, is a major pilgrimage site for devotees from around the world.

How to Reach

Puttaparthi is well-connected by road and rail. The nearest airport is Tirupati.
