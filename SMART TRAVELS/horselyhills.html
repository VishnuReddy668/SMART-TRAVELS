About Horsley Hills

Horsley Hills, a picturesque hill station in Andhra Pradesh, offers a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Nestled amidst lush greenery, this hill station is known for its pleasant climate, stunning views, and serene atmosphere.

Attractions

  • Malyalam Hills
  • Gangotri Lake
  • Horsley Hills Observatory
  • Nature Walks and Trekking
  • Sri Venkateswara Swamy Temple

How to Reach

Horsley Hills is well-connected by road. The nearest major railway station is Madanapalle.
