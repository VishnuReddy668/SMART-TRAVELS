About Kaigal Falls

Kaigal Falls, nestled amidst the lush greenery of the Eastern Ghats, is a hidden gem in Andhra Pradesh. This picturesque waterfall offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of city life, with its cascading waters and natural beauty.

Location

Kaigal Falls is located in the Chittoor district of Andhra Pradesh, approximately 100 kilometers from Tirupati. It is surrounded by verdant forests and offers a peaceful ambiance.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Kaigal Falls is during the monsoon season (June to September) when the waterfall is at its peak flow. The lush greenery and the roaring waters create a breathtaking spectacle.

How to Reach

Kaigal Falls is well-connected by road. The nearest major railway station is Tirupati.
